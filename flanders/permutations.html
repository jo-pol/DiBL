Permutations of stitches in a Flanders ground.

The table gives some information about how threads run though a sample of Flanders grounds with all permutations of the three basic stitches applied. More explanation and other types of stitches on the main page.

Don't have an absolute trust in the theory and algorithm that generated the table. Only some examples were checked.

Legend

X(Y/Z)
After X rows, Y colours and Z bobbins returned in their original position. In other words: Y out of 8 threads run more or less diagonal through the mesh, The other Z threads repeat their pattern each X rows.
