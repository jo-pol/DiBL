The examples with B-numbers match "Viele Gute Gründe" by Ulrike Löhr. The traditional Torchon grids cater for most of the "Gründe mit system" by Ute Ulrich, a selection of some special ones are represented by 4-digit numbers.

  • Basic Torchon / Flanders: B1
  • Torchon: Vierge, 3104, 3104; Honeycomb; spiders with 8, 12, 16 legs
  • Small snowflakes: B2
  • Binche/Duchesse: B3, B4-B6, B7
  • Rotated hexagons: with triangles, with circles, with triangles and circles

Please allow the javascript to download and execute, or try one of the links above.
