Bobbin lace working diagrams

Under construction: the data used for the generator based on templates needs a conversion to apply the same choices here.

  • Drag pins to fix their position, double-click to release.
  • Hover over the stitches or start symbols of threads or color coded pairs for tooltips.
  • The dark grey circles represent bobbins.
  • The diagrams might be rotated, mirrored or twisted: dragging can fix that. A fast move may (un)twist or flip a diagram. Two twists can become a flip too.
