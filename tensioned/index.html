  • The diagrams might be rotated, mirrored (see thread/pair numbers in diagram tooltips) or twisted: dragging can fix that.
  • FireFox, Chrome and Safari are fine, IE has a problem rendering the color codes of pair diagrams and over-under effect of thread diagrams.
  • Numbers of the NxM patterns are the same as for the interleaved patterns based on templates but different from the original data.
  • Most footsides of pair diagrams are inclomplete, that causes incomplete (and sometimes tubular) thread diagrams. Some configurations with complete footsides: snow, torchon, vrouwkens, bias. Other patterns without horizontal rows may render a triangular patch as thread diagram when specifying many columns.
