DiBL

A patchwork of sub-projects related to:
Diagrams for Bobbin Lace.

  • Plain as well as decorated polar grids
  • Choose stitches for computer generated grounds. Once you configured an appealing thread diagram, the matching color coded variant is only two clicks away.
  • Analyse the flow of threads in a generated ground: trace the threads on paper or use the thread style plug-in for InkScape to do the tracing.
  • More InkScape plug-ins for grids and grounds, a screen shot shows all dialogs together.
